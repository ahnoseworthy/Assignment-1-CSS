About The Song

The song is based upon a women reflecting on an ex-partner occasionally reaching out to her or being friendly in conversation with them. This brings upon a mental debate on whether or not their ex-partner still holds romantic feelings toward them. Though, contrary to the women's thoughts, the lyrics suggest that those actions don't necessarily mean that he wants her back. That if that was really the case, that he really wanted her back, there would be a clear effort to show that he wants her back with no doubts or misconceptions. The song lyrics go about encouraging women to keep their standards high and recognize that he isn't worth their time if he doesn't make a meaningful effort to be with you.

About The Artist

The artist Kylie Morgan is native to Oklahoma and is a country singer-songwriter who started writing music when she was only twelve years old. At 16 years old, she was included in the Country Music Associations list of who is new to watch and in 2019 was named one of CMT's Listen Up artists, which is a program that focuses on promising rising talent.
